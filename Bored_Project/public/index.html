Introduction

Have you found yourself doom scrolling on your phone? Maybe you're on your second hour watching cat videos on TikTok or yet another video of someone lambasting Salt Bae's restaurant. Either way, we get it. You're bored. Welcome the The Bored Solution. Here, we can help solve your boredom.

Here are the guidelines for generating an activity:

  1. Select a type of activity. These include "Education," "Recreational," "Social," and many more.
  2. Select a relative price range. Sliding the slider from left to right will result generate activities that are cheaper to more expensive.
  3. Select a the number of people you would like to involve in this activity.
  4. Select a relative accessibility range. Slider the slider from left to right will generate activities that are more accessible to less accessible.
  5. Hit the submit button!
  6. Sometimes there may not be an activity that fits all of your desired conditions. If that is the case, try mixing around with new choices and generate another activity.
